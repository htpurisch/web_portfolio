Hi everyone! I am an Undergraduate human geographer currently studying at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am interested in critical theory and analyses of science and technology and their roles in cultural reproduction, and the bearings predominant understadings of science have on us as political subjects. I am currently working on my Senior Honors thesis on the Indigenous Resurgence movement and how theories of perfomativity might add to political theories of Indigenous Resurgence.

In my free time I enjoy riding my bike, reading poems and books, watching movies, and practicing my German!
